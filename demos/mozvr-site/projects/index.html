Projects

This is our full collection of virtual reality web projects, experiments, demos, etc. To enter the VR mode of these projects, be sure to use a VR-enabled browser, likeFirefox with VR and a compatible VR headset.

Monkeys

A weird and wonderful visualization of seamlessly-linked primates.

Inspirit

A surreal interactive audio/visual experience.

Rainbow Membrane

A motion-controlled VR sensory experience with Leap Motion and WebGL.

Sechelt

A visualization of British Columbia's coast built with three.js and Cinema 4D.

Panorama Viewer

A panorama viewer made from equirectangular images mapped to a WebGL sphere that surrounds the user.

The Polar Sea

A documentary by DEEP that takes users to the Arctic in 360° VR video, powered by eleVR’s WebGL VR video player.
